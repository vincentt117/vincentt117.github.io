Franklin

A web application built on MEAN (Mongo DB, Express, Angular JS, Node.JS) Stack for use by Venture Capitalist firm Coventure during the deCODE 2016 Ottawa Hackathon. Developed in under 24 hours, Franklin allowed investment firms to track the progress of start-ups they have invested in through custom KPI questions that formulate visual data representations. The KPI can request financial and other performance metric data with data ranges and other specifications.
